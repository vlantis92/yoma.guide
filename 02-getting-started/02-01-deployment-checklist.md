# Deployment Checklist

> **Outline:**
> - Pre-launch checklist covering all three marketplace sides: Opportunities, Rewards, and Youth

> - Platform prerequisites: organisation registered, admin access granted, staging tested
> - Operational prerequisites: team in place, points economy designed, comms materials ready
> - Go/no-go criteria for launching in a new market
